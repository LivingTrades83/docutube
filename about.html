What's new in DocuTube?

  • Search! Stop leaving your doc to find the video you want.
  • Embed! You can now embed the video thumbnail or title right into your document (at the cursor location).
  • Copy! If you don't want to embed, you can copy the link right to your clipboard.
  • Watch! All videos are now loaded at the same time - no more choosing between the doc or comments.

See more on the DocuTube home page.
